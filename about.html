About Sentryx

Your Owner's Representative, On the Ground

Sentryx is a private property oversight and estate management company built for owners who keep their Rockland, Westchester, or Orange County property as a second home.

Our Name

Sentry. Intelligent. Watching Over What Matters.

The name Sentryx comes from "Sentry" — a guard, a watchful presence — combined with "X," representing something more advanced and intelligent. Together, it reflects exactly what we do: intelligent property protection and oversight for homeowners who can't always be there themselves.

Rockland, Westchester, and Orange Counties are home to many large estates that owners keep as second homes — visited seasonally, not lived in full-time. Sentryx was created to give those owners a trusted, local presence at their property — someone who treats the home as carefully as the owner would themselves.

A

Acting as Your Representative

We serve as your on-the-ground point of contact, so you don't have to manage anything directly.

Z

Handling the Home A to Z

From routine checks to emergency coordination, Sentryx takes ownership of the details.

Our Approach

Premium Care, Without the Worry

Sentryx is not a handyman service, a cleaning company, or an alarm monitoring provider. We're a dedicated layer of oversight built specifically for owners who value privacy and peace of mind.

Local & Hands-On

We're on the ground in Rockland, Westchester, and Orange Counties, not managing your property from a call center.

Complete Transparency

Clear reporting after every visit, so you always know the state of your property.

Trusted & Vetted

Every vendor and contractor we coordinate is vetted before they set foot on your property.

Discreet & Private

Your estate, your business. We operate quietly and respect your privacy at all times.

Where We Work

Sentryx serves Rockland, Westchester, and Orange Counties, with a focus on second-home and estate owners who need a trusted representative close to home.
