About Us

Built to Endure, Not to Impress

SentryxEstates is a privately held real estate investment firm focused on acquiring, developing, and managing institutional-quality assets across the United States.

Our Story

Two Decades of Disciplined Investing

SentryxEstates was founded on a simple premise: real estate returns are made through disciplined underwriting and hands-on management, not market timing. What began as a small group of investors acquiring overlooked assets in secondary markets has grown into a firm managing a diversified portfolio across office, multifamily, industrial, and retail properties nationwide.

Today, our team combines deep local market knowledge with institutional-grade processes, allowing us to move quickly on opportunities while maintaining the rigor our investors expect.

2007

SentryxEstates founded with its first acquisition, a 60-unit multifamily property.

2013

Expanded into industrial and office acquisitions, entering five new metro markets.

2018

Launched a dedicated development platform for ground-up and adaptive-reuse projects.

2024

Surpassed $1.2B in assets under management across 14 markets nationwide.
