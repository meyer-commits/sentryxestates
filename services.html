Our Services

Everything Your Home Needs, Handled

Sentryx takes care of your home from A to Z, so you can enjoy ownership without the worry.

Home Oversight & Watch

Regular, scheduled visits to your home so nothing goes unnoticed while you're away.

  • Scheduled interior and exterior walkthroughs
  • Security and safety checks
  • Weather and seasonal monitoring
  • Detailed reporting after every visit

Home Visit Preparation

Your home prepared and welcoming before you arrive, every time.

  • Heating and cooling adjusted ahead of your arrival
  • Lights, water, and utilities checked
  • Cleaning coordinated before your visit
  • A fresh, welcoming home every time

Vendor & Maintenance Coordination

We manage the people who take care of your home, so you don't have to.

  • Scheduling landscapers, cleaners, and contractors
  • On-site supervision of vendor visits
  • Ongoing maintenance tracking across the home
  • Trusted, vetted local providers only

Emergency Response Coordination

When something goes wrong, we're the first call. Our team's background as active first responders means we stay calm, take charge, and see it through.

  • Immediate response to alarms and alerts
  • Coordination of emergency repairs
  • Storm and severe weather follow-up
  • Direct communication with you throughout

Owner Representation

Above all, Sentryx acts as your representative at the home — the umbrella that ties every other service together.

  • One point of contact for everything at your home
  • Decisions made with your best interest in mind
  • Complete transparency into everything we handle
  • A trusted representative — not a call center

Not sure which services you need?

Every home is different. Let's talk through what yours needs.
