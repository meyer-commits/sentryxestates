How It Works

From First Call to Complete Peace of Mind

Here's exactly what to expect when you bring Sentryx on to look after your property.

01 — Complimentary Consultation & Walkthrough

We meet you at the property to understand your home, your concerns, and your schedule — no obligation required.

02 — Customized Oversight Plan

Based on the walkthrough, we build a plan around check frequency, vendor needs, and any special instructions specific to your estate.

03 — Ongoing Property Checks

Scheduled visits inside and out, with reporting after each one, so you always know the state of your home.

04 — Vendor & Maintenance Coordination

We schedule, meet, and supervise any landscapers, cleaners, or contractors your property needs.

05 — Home Visit Preparation

Ahead of your arrival, we make sure the home is heated or cooled, cleaned, and ready to welcome you.

06 — Always On Call

If something happens while you're away — an alarm, a storm, a maintenance issue — we respond immediately and keep you informed.

No Set-Up Hassle, No Long Contracts

Every plan is built around your specific property and schedule — starting with a no-obligation walkthrough.
